Understanding SHAP

Positive (+ Red) values pull the prediction towards a higher academic risk classification. Lower study hours and excessive absences represent standard risk-increasing variables.

Negative (- Green) values represent stabilizing factors that reduce the likelihood of academic dropouts, such as extracurricular student support hours.
